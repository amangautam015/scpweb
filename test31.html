ONLINE COURSE RESOURCES

Open Course Ware (OCW) database has been prepared and manged by senior master's students. They have provided links to useful resources like lecture notes and videos which they feel might help the juniors to handle the courses. As the database will be updated continuously this will always be a work in progress. Please make full use of the opportunity and provide feedback to us about this initiative.
To Seniors - If you'd like to help us in this initiative (either as co-contributor to following subjects or if you want to handle a new subject) please drop a mail to amangautam1501@gmail.com Or +91 704 580 0502.
To All - If you want to us to cover some subject which is not in the list please contact at the mail ids given above.
